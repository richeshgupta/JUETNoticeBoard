Q. What (a) depicts?

Q. How the donation money will be used?

Donation money will be used to cover our hosting charges and other operational charges, like paying for third party apis (if any) but we try our level best that what we do is always cost efficient.

Q. What happens when i report any question/answer?

A object is created for our moderators that you have reported this question/answer and how many other reports have came in. If after a threshold times there's report of any content we check it, that if it's infringing our community guidelines we remove the content.

Note that when question is removed the respective answers of that question is automatically removed too.


Q. What is PantoMath?

PantoMath is a group of people who have vision to provide all the technological solution to the problem that exist in our college with solely help of students of this college to practice production like scenerios to learn better.

Q. How can i help PantoMath or get into it?

Contribute on our open source projects and we'll give shoutout to you and enough. You are in!

Q. What if i have something to discuss with admins of this website?

You can e-mail us at pantomath.notice@gmail.com with subject URGENT.

Q. How to choose right tags for my question/answer?

Think like if you needed to describe your content in two words what would they be, these tags will later be used to search content on this website.
